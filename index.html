Meet the Groomer

Angela Benton

Owner & Lead Dog Groomer

Angela Benton brings passion, patience, and professional expertise to every dog that walks through the door at Heads & Tails. With years of hands-on grooming experience, she treats every pup like her own.

Angela built Heads & Tails on the belief that grooming should be a positive, stress-free experience for dogs and their owners alike. Her calm, gentle approach puts even the most anxious pups at ease, and her attention to detail ensures every dog leaves looking and feeling their absolute best.

Dogs only. No cats — because Angela believes in doing one thing and doing it exceptionally well.
